Privacy Policy for Moodify
Last Updated: April 20, 2026

At Moodify, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect
Moodify is a private mood tracking application. To provide our services, we collect:

Authentication Data: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture URL.
App Data: Your mood entries, notes, pet stats, and milestone progress.
Device Information: Basic technical information (OS version, device model) for crash reporting and app performance.
2. How We Use Your Data
Personalization: To display your name and profile in the app.
Syncing: We use Google Firebase to securely sync your data across devices.
Gamification: To manage leaderboards and rewards like XP and Coins.
No Monetization: We NEVER sell, trade, or rent your personal mood logs or diary entries to third parties for advertising.
3. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase.

Encryption: Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
Ownership: You retain 100% copyright and ownership of your personal reflections and entries.
4. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own policies:

Google Play Services
Firebase Authentication
Cloud Firestore
Google Analytics for Firebase
5. User Rights & Data Deletion
You have absolute sovereignty over your data:

Access: You can view all your logs within the app.
Deletion: You can permanently delete your mood entries or your entire account at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.
6. Community Safety
Features like the Royal Shoutout are subject to our Community Honor Code. Usage of inappropriate language or abuse may result in permanent account suspension.
